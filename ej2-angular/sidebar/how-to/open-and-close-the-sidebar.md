# Open and close the Angular Sidebar component

Opening and closing the Sidebar can be achieved using built-in public methods.

* [`show()`](../../api/sidebar/#show): Method to open the Sidebar.
* [`hide()`](../../api/sidebar/#hide): Method to close the Sidebar.
* [`toggle()`](../../api/sidebar/#toggle): Method to toggle between the open and close states of the Sidebar.

In the following sample, the toggle method has been used to show or hide the Sidebar on button click.
